Our Team

Meet the team that is changing the game.

Style is as unique as the individual who wears it, which is why Blohaute hires an eclectic mix of personalities to fit the style you’re aiming for. Amanda

Founder and CEO

Based in Chicago, Diedrich owns and manages the Blohaute business, offering women stunning hair and beauty services in the comfort and convenience of their own home or office.

After earning her cosmetology license from Tricoci University, Diedrich honed her skills as a Stylist at Asha SalonSpa and as a Colorist at Mario Tricoci. She is also certified in fusion and tape-in extensions. Her eye for beauty and patient, perfectionistic hands allow Diedrich to create her own unique take on trending hairstyles, leading the curve for hairstyle innovation and creativity.

Mandy

Stylist & Makeup Artist

As another one of Blohaute's top stylists, Mandy has a true eye for hair. Gaining her education from Paul Mitchell Cosmetology School right here in Chicago, Mandy is filled with creativity.

Specializing in blowouts & upstyles, Mandy is one of our Blohaute Braiding Queens!

April

Stylist

As one of the Lead Stylists for Blohaute, April has over 12 years of experience in the hair & beauty industry. Graduating from Kent Career Technical Institue with her Cosmetology License in 2002, April started her career in the beauty industry as a Cosmetology Instructor at Excel Academy in Grand Rapids, MI.

Now a freelance hairstylist for Blohaute, April's previous work has been featured in Chicago Style Weddings, CS Magazine, and TCW Magazine. With an extreme passion for styling, April specializes in modern/classic upstyles and vintage/California waves under specialties for special events and weddings.
